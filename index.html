End-of-year project: 2D platformer

The aim of this project is to design a 2D side-scroller on my own within three months.
This is what I came up with: You play as a laboratory rabbit who, after the lab falls into ruin, wants to escape to regain his freedom. However, he discovers that the researchers have managed to grant him powers, such as the ability to make the world spin around him. GitHub !
